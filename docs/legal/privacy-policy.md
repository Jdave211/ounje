Ounje helps you turn recipes, preferences, schedules, and grocery needs into meal prep plans and shopping workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information Ounje collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## Information we collect

### Account information

We collect information you provide when you create or use an account, such as your email address, authentication details, account identifiers, and app settings.

### Onboarding and profile information

We collect meal planning preferences you give us, including cuisines, allergies, dietary restrictions, household size, prep rhythm, recipes per prep, grocery budget, delivery preferences, delivery address, delivery instructions, and checkout/autopilot preferences.

### Recipe, meal prep, and grocery information

We collect the information needed to build and manage your Ounje experience, including saved recipes, imported recipe links, recipe text, images or videos you share into Ounje, generated meal prep plans, recurring recipes, grocery lists, cart items, ingredient substitutions, recipe ratings, prep history, and grocery order status.

### Provider connection and automation information

If you connect or use a grocery provider, such as Instacart or another supported provider, we may process the information needed to prepare carts, run checkout-related automation, track order progress, and show status in Ounje. This can include provider session status, cart contents, run logs, checkout preferences, and order metadata.

Ounje does not need your grocery provider password unless a specific provider flow requires you to enter credentials directly with that provider. Do not send provider passwords to Ounje support.

### Device, usage, and diagnostics

We collect basic technical information needed to operate and secure the app, such as device type, app version, operating system version, crash or error logs, request metadata, performance events, and feature usage events.

### Purchases and entitlements

If you buy a subscription or in-app purchase through Apple, Apple processes your payment. We receive subscription and entitlement information needed to unlock paid features, but we do not receive your full card number.

### Communications

If you contact us, we collect the information you send, such as your email address, support messages, screenshots, and related account or device details.

## How we use information

We use information to:

- create and manage your account;
- generate, regenerate, and personalize meal prep plans;
- import, parse, save, and organize recipes;
- build grocery lists and carts;
- support provider connections, checkout workflows, and order status;
- remember preferences like cuisines, allergies, budget, household size, prep rhythm, and delivery instructions;
- improve search, discovery, grocery canonicalization, and recipe recommendations;
- provide support, debug issues, and send service messages;
- process subscriptions and entitlements;
- protect the app, prevent abuse, and maintain reliability;
- comply with legal obligations.

## AI features

Ounje uses AI systems to help parse recipes, generate meal prep plans, recommend recipes, organize grocery items, and improve search and discovery. Information you provide may be sent to AI service providers so they can return the requested result.

AI output can be wrong. You are responsible for reviewing recipes, ingredients, quantities, allergens, nutrition information, grocery carts, substitutions, delivery details, and checkout actions before relying on them.

## Allergies, dietary needs, and health information

Ounje may use allergy and dietary preference information to personalize suggestions and reduce unsuitable recommendations. Ounje is not medical, nutrition, allergy, or health advice. Always verify ingredient labels, packaging, restaurant or grocery information, and cross-contamination risks yourself.

## How we share information

We may share information with:

- infrastructure providers that host Ounje and store app data;
- database, authentication, and realtime service providers;
- AI providers that process recipe, grocery, search, and meal planning requests;
- Apple, for purchases, subscriptions, App Store distribution, crash reporting, and platform services;
- grocery providers and automation providers when you choose to connect, build carts, or run checkout-related workflows;
- support, security, logging, and diagnostics providers;
- professional advisers, legal authorities, or regulators when required or appropriate;
- a buyer, successor, or affiliate if Ounje is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or asset sale.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your personal information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking. If that changes, we will update this Privacy Policy and any required App Store privacy disclosures.

## Third-party services

Ounje may link to or interact with third-party services, including grocery providers, recipe websites, Apple services, and payment or subscription systems. Their privacy policies and terms apply to their services. Ounje is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

## Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide Ounje, maintain your account, support meal prep and grocery history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and enforce our terms. We may retain de-identified, aggregated, or backup information where permitted by law.

## Your choices

You can update many preferences in the app, including meal planning, household, budget, and grocery settings.

You can disconnect provider connections where supported.

You can manage subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

You can request account deletion or data deletion by using the in-app account deletion flow, if available, or by contacting thisisounje@gmail.com. When you delete your account, we will delete or de-identify your account information, profile, recipes, meal prep plans, grocery data, provider connection data, and related app records unless we need to retain information for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons.

## Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
