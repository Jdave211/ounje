# Ounje Support

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Need help with Ounje? Contact us at thisisounje@gmail.com.

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

## What to include

To help us fix the issue faster, include:

- the email address on your Ounje account;
- your iPhone model and iOS version;
- your Ounje app version;
- screenshots or a screen recording, if useful;
- the recipe link, shared post, meal prep, cart, or order involved;
- the time the issue happened.

Do not send grocery provider passwords, payment card numbers, or one-time login codes.

## Recipe import and sharing

To import a recipe or food video, open the source app or website, tap Share, then choose Ounje.

If Ounje is not shown, tap More, choose Ounje, and favorite it so it appears sooner next time.

## Meal prep generation

If a prep is taking longer than expected, keep Ounje open until the loading state finishes. If it fails, try again once. If it keeps failing, contact support with the prep name, time, and any error shown.

## Grocery lists and carts

If a grocery item looks duplicated, missing, or incorrectly grouped, send us a screenshot and the related recipe or prep. Ounje groups grocery items from multiple recipes, and your report helps us improve matching.

## Grocery provider or checkout issues

Ounje can help prepare carts and run supported grocery workflows, but provider prices, inventory, substitutions, delivery windows, refunds, and account issues are handled by the grocery provider. For order charges, delivery issues, missing items, or refunds, contact the grocery provider directly.

If Ounje prepared the wrong cart or showed the wrong status, contact Ounje support with the run time, provider, and screenshots.

## Subscriptions and billing

If you subscribed through Apple, billing, cancellation, and refunds are managed by Apple.

To manage a subscription on iPhone:

1. Open Settings.
2. Tap your Apple Account.
3. Tap Subscriptions.
4. Choose Ounje.

For refund requests, use Apple's refund flow.

## Account deletion

You can request account deletion in the app, if available, or by emailing thisisounje@gmail.com from the email address on your Ounje account.

Account deletion removes or de-identifies your account, profile, recipes, meal prep plans, grocery data, provider connection data, and related app records unless we need to retain information for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons.

## Safety and allergies

Ounje is not medical, nutrition, allergy, or dietetic advice. Always verify ingredients, labels, substitutions, cross-contamination risks, and nutrition details yourself before cooking or purchasing.

If you see a serious allergy or safety issue in Ounje output, email thisisounje@gmail.com with "Safety" in the subject line.
