# Ounje Onboarding Online Asset Shortlist

The onboarding should stay native to Ounje's dark app theme. Use outside assets as small motion accents inside dark cards, not as bright full-screen illustrations.

## Licensing Guardrails

- Prefer LottieFiles free animations. LottieFiles says free animations use the Lottie Simple License, including commercial app use, but each animation page should still be checked before adding it.
- Flaticon and similar icon libraries are useful for visual search, but do not ship those assets unless the specific pack license is confirmed. Free Flaticon assets usually need attribution unless covered by a paid plan.
- Google Images is only a discovery tool. Follow through to the original source and verify usage rights before embedding anything in the app.
- Keep SF Symbols as the default fallback for functional UI icons. Replace only the hero/accent slots where motion adds clear value.

## Page-by-Page Asset Targets

### 1. Name / Meet Ounje
- Placement: top coach panel accent.
- Search: [LottieFiles cooking coach](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=cooking%20coach&category=animations), [LottieFiles recipe app](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=recipe%20app&category=animations).
- Direction: a small food assistant, chef, or recipe checklist motion. It should read clearly at about 96-120 pt on a dark panel.

### 2. Guardrails
- Placement: food rules card.
- Search: [LottieFiles food safety](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=food%20safety&category=animations), [Flaticon allergy food](https://www.flaticon.com/search?word=food%20allergy).
- Direction: shield, allergy, lock, or checklist. Avoid medical-looking assets.

### 3. Taste Lane
- Placement: cuisine picker card.
- Search: [LottieFiles food choice](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=food%20choice&category=animations), [Flaticon cuisine icons](https://www.flaticon.com/search?word=cuisine).
- Direction: a compact plate grid, food map, or flavor-choice animation. Keep cuisine flags/text out of the asset so localization stays in SwiftUI.

### 4. Prep Rhythm
- Placement: cadence and meals-per-week card.
- Search: [LottieFiles meal planner](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=meal%20planner&category=animations), [LottieFiles calendar food](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=calendar%20food&category=animations).
- Direction: calendar, recurring arrows, stacked recipe cards.

### 5. Kitchen Check
- Placement: equipment selector card.
- Search: [LottieFiles kitchen tools](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=kitchen%20tools&category=animations), [Flaticon kitchen appliances](https://www.flaticon.com/search?word=kitchen%20appliance).
- Direction: clean appliance/tool set. Static icons are enough here unless a very subtle Lottie fits.

### 6. Budget / Household
- Placement: budget target card.
- Search: [LottieFiles grocery budget](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=grocery%20budget&category=animations), [LottieFiles shopping cart money](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=shopping%20cart%20money&category=animations).
- Direction: cart + price tag, budget slider, or receipt. No finance-dashboard visuals.

### 7. Autopilot
- Placement: checkout autonomy card.
- Search: [LottieFiles grocery delivery](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=grocery%20delivery&category=animations), [LottieFiles shopping delivery](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=shopping%20delivery&category=animations).
- Direction: delivery bag, route, approval check. Should feel controlled, not fully driverless.

### 8. Ready
- Placement: final confirmation card.
- Search: [LottieFiles success food](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=success%20food&category=animations), [LottieFiles checklist complete](https://lottiefiles.com/search?q=checklist%20complete&category=animations).
- Direction: checkmark, recipe cards, grocery basket. Short loop or one-shot success works best.
